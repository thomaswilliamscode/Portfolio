✨ Hello World!
I wasn’t born with a superpower, I earned mine.

“A man can change his stars.” – A Knight’s Tale

There was a time when I tried every shortcut. Uber. Truck driving. Even writing and YouTube. I was searching for something that felt right, but deep down, I was avoiding hard work because I was afraid I wasn’t enough.

Funny thing is… I already knew what I loved.

Back in high school, I took a programming class. I even had a paying client. But after a rough experience, I gave up. Still, that little spark never left me.

Years later, when I finally got tired of cutting corners, I went back to coding. No shortcuts. No excuses.

I started with Python. Writing little games on Replit using borrowed Wi-Fi. Then I leveled up to JavaScript, React, Redux, Express, and more. It was hard. I failed A lot. I learned without a computer. I dropped out of a coding bootcamp. I rebuilt my confidence on Treehouse, YouTube, and trial by fire.

Eventually, I earned my certificate from BloomTech, and I got a whole new sense of who I am.

🛠 Tools I Use (and Love)

  • Python – my first language and future ML curiosity
  • JavaScript – my main weapon, hard-won and battle-tested
  • React + Redux – I love thinking in components and wrangling data
  • Express + SQLite3 – backend tools that feel like puzzle pieces clicking into place

🌱 What I'm Looking For

My dream role? Somewhere I can grow, build, and give back. I’d love to work with a team that’s passionate, supportive, and open to new ideas. Bonus points if it’s related to animals, education, or anything that genuinely makes the world a little better.

I don’t need micromanagement. I need trust, purpose, and a chance to shine.

💬 Let’s Connect

If you’re looking for a developer who’s walked the long road and still believes in magic, I’d love to hear from you. Let’s build something meaningful together. And just maybe we can change our stars together. ⭐
