Hello World!
I wasn’t born with a superpower, I earned mine.

There was a time when I was quirkless—just a guy in his late 20s with a library card, no computer, and a dream too big to fit in a warehouse shift.

I spent my spare time grinding—writing Python scripts on borrowed Wi-Fi and teaching myself from library books. I failed, started over, failed, and tried again. Still I felt like I wasn’t enough. But just like every great hero origin story… something changed.

I trained. I learned. I leveled up.

I learned to wield JavaScript through sheer will and stubborn grit. React, Redux, Express, and SQLite—they’re more than tools. They’re my superpowers.

And I’m just getting started.

Welcome to my portfolio. Let’s build something heroic together.
